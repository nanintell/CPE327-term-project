FAQ

Q: How far in advance can I book my tickets?

A: A cinema week is from Thursday to Wednesday. To better meet the needs of our patrons, please refer to our Programming schedule: Thursday to Friday sessions (released on Wednesday after 3pm) / Saturday to Wednesday sessions (released on Friday after 3pm)

Q: What should I do if I lose my tickets or make a wrong booking?

A: Wrong booking or Changes for regular hall please proceed to the nearest GV cinema at least 1 hour before the movie session to seek assistance from staff.
Wrong booking or Changes for Gold Class hall please proceed to the nearest Gold Class cinema at least 1 hour before the movie session to seek assistance from staff.

Q: I have a complimentary ticket/pass/movie gift voucher, how do I book my tickets?

A: Please proceed to the Box Office to exchange your complimentary ticket/pass/movie gift voucher for a movie ticket.

Q: Why can't I select seats by leaving a space in between?

A: The purpose is to optimize seating arrangements in the cinema hall so that patrons in group will not have to be separated especially when the occupancy is high.
